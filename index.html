How to Deposit USDT (TRC-20)

Follow these steps to deposit USDT to your PENATA account:

  1. With a Binance Account:
    • Log in to Binance.
    • Go to "Wallet" > "Spot Wallet" and search for USDT.
    • Click "Withdraw," select "TRC-20" network, and paste the address above.
    • Enter the amount (e.g., 10, 15, 18, 36, or 72 USDT), confirm, and complete the transaction.
    • Copy the Transaction ID (TxID) after sending and paste it in the form above.
  2. Without a Binance Account:
    • We recommend creating a Binance account for the easiest and safest way to deposit USDT.
    • Once registered, fund your account via P2P trading or card payment, then follow the steps above.
    • Alternatively, ask a friend with a Binance account to send USDT to the address above and provide you with the Transaction ID.

Note: Ensure the amount matches a plan (10, 15, 18, 36, 72 USDT). Deposits take 5-30 minutes to reflect after network confirmation and admin approval.
